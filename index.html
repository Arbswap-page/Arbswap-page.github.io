Introduction to Arbswap

Arbswap is a decentralized, high-performance cross-chain protocol that facilitates permissionless, secure, and rapid transfer of digital assets across major blockchain networks such as Ethereum, BNB Chain, and Arbitrum. As an integral component of the growing DeFi infrastructure, Arbswap ensures users maintain custody over their funds while reducing bridging costs and complexities.

What is Arbswap?

Arbswap is a next-generation non-custodial bridge that offers seamless cross-chain interoperability. It eliminates the need for centralized intermediaries by employing decentralized validators, audited smart contracts, and a transparent on-chain governance structure.

  • Gas-optimized bridging between multiple networks
  • Fast finality and minimal downtime
  • Full decentralization and non-custodial asset management
  • Smart contract audits and real-time transaction monitoring

Why Arbswap Stands Out

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Use Arbswap

  1. Connect Wallet: Use MetaMask, WalletConnect, or a hardware wallet.
  2. Select Networks: Pick source and destination blockchains.
  3. Choose Token: Select the asset and input amount.
  4. Approve Asset: Confirm token allowance in wallet.
  5. Confirm Transaction: Validate the bridge operation on-chain.
  6. Track Status: Use the dashboard to view confirmations.

    • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) – Arbswap

    Is Arbswap Safe?

    Yes. Arbswap is audited by third-party firms and relies on a decentralized set of validators to process all transactions.

    What Assets Can I Bridge?

    You can currently bridge ETH, USDT, USDC, ARB, and other supported tokens.

    How Long Does a Transfer Take?

    Typically, transfers are finalized within 3–10 minutes depending on network congestion.

    Are There Any Fees?

    Yes, but fees are transparent and shown before confirming your transaction.

    Can I Use a Hardware Wallet?

    Yes, Arbswap supports Ledger and other Web3-compatible hardware wallets.

    Where Can I Check the Status of My Transaction?

    The dashboard provides real-time status updates and explorer links for every transaction.
